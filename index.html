Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my Friends. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.

Black Widow
Good production

It involves strong and often emotional dialogues, it shows Natasha and even Yelena suffering through trauma of their past, they stay strong with it all until they eventually break and share with their family. In the dinner-table sequence, the opening credits, and the gas station sister conversation, they let each other free up and let their buried thoughts free.Scarlett Johansson as Natasha Romanoff is as always, a strong, brave, humble-at-heart, touching character. Florence Pugh as Yelena Belova sets new ground, her character is naive and also humble-at-heart. The sisterhood between them touches your heart. Rachel Weisz as Melina Vostokoff was pretty good too, she wasn't present for all too long but she made her impact as the mother well enough, she helps build Nat and Yelena as people with her quote 'Pain only makes you stronger' being continuously revised by them throughout the film. David Harbour as Alexei Shostakov (The Red Guardian) was quite a joy, he lightens up the movie right before it gets way too deep or emotional, he's charismatic with his role and keeps the mood up while also representing a bold, strong and fierce man, who eventually chooses to fight for his family as long as he could (fresh from prison, lack of physical activity and practice lead to weak stamina). Olga Kurylenko was an interesting choice for Antonia Dreykov (Taskmaster), she barely had an appearance, but she was part of Nat's past that troubled her, a past she seeks to (and successfully managed to) settle down and fix. Taskmaster's theme is DARK AND DEEP. It's one of the strongest villains' themes I've heard, it's threatening and dark, fits perfectly, and gives me chills with her suit and theme together. Would gladly love to see Olga as Antonia (Taskmaster) again in any MCU production.

    The perceived scale of the Amazon blazes received global attention this summer. However, international concerns raised at the time were countered by the Brazilian Government, which claimed the fire situation in August was 'normal' and 'below the historical average'.An international team of scientists writing in the journal Global Change Biology say the number of active fires in August was actually three times higher than in 2018 and the highest number since 2010.
